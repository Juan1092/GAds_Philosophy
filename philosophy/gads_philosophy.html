Google Ads Campaign Philosophy

Summary Focus
Go for Big Money Service — prioritize campaigns, keywords, and budgets around the highest-value legal services that drive meaningful revenue.
Phase 1
1
VISIBILITY
Primary Goal
Generate impressions and clicks to gather initial data
Key KPIs
Impressions Clicks CTR Reach

Key Actions

  • Launch the campaign using phrase and exact match keywords to optimize traffic quality from the start
  • Establish a baseline test budget
  • Monitor ad visibility and reach to ensure proper delivery
  • Identify high-potential, high-value ("big money") keywords for future scaling
Phase 2
2
QUALITY
Primary Goal
Improve lead quality while maintaining click volume
Key KPIs
Traffic Quality Lead Quality Conversion Rate Time on Site Bounce Rate

Key Actions

  • Tighten targeting around high-intent, high-value keywords
  • Exclude keywords that attract low-quality or irrelevant traffic
  • Maintain stable click volume and consistent spend levels
  • Analyze user engagement patterns to isolate profitable traffic sources
  • Refine ad copy and creatives to attract premium-value clients aligned with "big money services"
Phase 3
3
EFFICIENCY
Primary Goal
Reduce cost per lead while increasing total lead volume
Key KPIs
CPL Conversion Volume

Key Actions

  • Optimize CPC bids to balance cost and scalability
  • Expand budget allocation on proven high-performing segments
  • Maintain continuous A/B testing and strict cost control to improve ROI
